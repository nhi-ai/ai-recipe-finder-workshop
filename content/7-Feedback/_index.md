### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
Throughout my internship, I had the opportunity to experience a professional and well-organized working environment. Every team member demonstrated a strong sense of responsibility and ownership while remaining approachable and willing to support others whenever needed. The structured workflow—from task assignment and progress tracking to project reviews—helped me develop better discipline, time management, and professional working habits. Overall, the environment provided an excellent foundation for students to gain practical industry experience.

**2. Support from Mentors and Team Administrators**  
One of the most valuable aspects of this internship was the continuous guidance provided by the mentors and team administrators. Whenever I encountered technical challenges, they patiently explained not only the solutions but also the reasoning behind them. Instead of simply providing answers, they encouraged me to analyze problems independently and guided me toward the most appropriate approach. Their willingness to share technical knowledge, industry experience, and career advice greatly contributed to both my professional growth and confidence.

**3. Relevance to My Major**  
The internship tasks closely aligned with my academic background in Information Technology. During the project, I was involved in multiple stages of software development, including requirement analysis, UI development, API integration, cloud deployment, testing, and system optimization. This experience allowed me to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge learned at university and practical software engineering in a real-world environment.

**4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities**  
This internship significantly enhanced both my technical and soft skills. Besides improving my programming abilities and gaining hands-on experience with AWS cloud services, I also strengthened my skills in technical research, reading English documentation, teamwork, communication, and problem-solving. Working on a real project taught me how to approach challenges systematically and continuously improve through feedback from mentors and teammates.

**5. Team Culture and Collaboration**  
The team maintained an open, collaborative, and respectful working culture. Every member was encouraged to share ideas and participate in discussions, regardless of their role or experience level. As an intern, I felt comfortable expressing my opinions and contributing to project discussions. This supportive atmosphere motivated me to become more proactive, communicate more effectively, and continuously improve my technical capabilities.

**6. Internship Program and Benefits**  
The internship program was designed with students in mind, offering a flexible schedule that made it possible to balance academic responsibilities with project work. In addition to participating in real software development, interns were given opportunities to attend workshops, technical sharing sessions, and community events. These experiences broadened my understanding of cloud computing and software development while providing valuable exposure to industry practices.

### Additional Questions

- **What was the most satisfying part of your internship?**

The most rewarding experience was contributing to the development of a real software project from start to finish. Being involved in each phase of the development process allowed me to gain practical experience that cannot be obtained through classroom learning alone. I also appreciated the opportunity to work alongside experienced mentors who continuously encouraged my growth.

- **What do you think the company could improve for future interns?**

Overall, I believe the internship program is well organized. If possible, I would recommend adding more in-depth technical workshops, project review sessions, or career-sharing events where interns can interact directly with experienced engineers. These activities would provide additional opportunities for learning and professional development.

- **Would you recommend this internship program to your friends? Why?**

Absolutely. I would highly recommend this internship to students who want to gain practical experience in software development and cloud technologies. The program offers meaningful real-world projects, supportive mentors, and an environment that encourages continuous learning and professional growth.

### Suggestions and Future Expectations

- **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**

I hope the program can continue expanding opportunities for interns to participate in technical discussions, knowledge-sharing sessions, and collaborative project activities. Additional mentorship sessions focusing on career development and industry best practices would also be highly beneficial for students preparing to enter the workforce.

- **Would you like to continue participating in this program in the future?**

Yes. Given the opportunity, I would be delighted to continue participating in future projects or become part of the program after graduation. I believe this environment provides excellent opportunities to further develop my technical expertise, gain valuable industry experience, and contribute to meaningful software projects.
