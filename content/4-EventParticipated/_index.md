Before the end of my internship journey at First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ), what I treasure most is not only the technical knowledge I gained, but also the series of activities the program organized for interns. Through each event, I had the opportunity to connect with industry professionals, listen to real-world experiences, and explore new perspectives on technology and career development. Among all the activities I participated in, three events left the most significant impression on me. Each brought its own unique value from practical lessons and memorable experiences to small but meaningful gifts all contributing to an internship filled with memories and motivation to keep growing.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)
&emsp;**Event Name:** AWS FIRST CLOUD AI JOURNEY COMMUNITY DAY

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 9:00 AM, Saturday, May 23, 2026 

&emsp;**Location:** 36th Floor, Bitexco Tower, No. 02 Hai Trieu Street, Sai Gon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City 

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee 

&emsp;**Event Description:** The event was organized to provide the IT community with an updated perspective on the development of cloud computing and generative AI. Speakers shared practical insights on building specialized AI Agents, advanced techniques for improving LLM interaction quality, and solutions for securing network infrastructure on the AWS platform in enterprise environments.
